Heart of Darkness

By Joseph Conrad

Heart of Darkness was published in 1899 by Polish-English writer Joeseph Conrad.
The novel contains grueling testimates of human endurance and empathy as well as the terrible
toll of European imperialism in the 1800's. The vivid descriptions of the landscapes used thr-
oughout the novella create an incredible sense of despair and distrust that is juxtaposed by an urge to
dive further into the murky environment our lead describes.

It stands to be one of, if not the best of Conrad's works throughout his acclaimed catalogue.
